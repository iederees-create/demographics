Subscription Plans - Target Market Analysis

Target Market: High school students, pre-law undergraduates, career changers.

Demographics:

  • Age: 16-25
  • Location: Urban/Suburban
  • Income: Low-Moderate
  • Interests: Career Exploration, Law School Prep

Target Market: Law school students, recent graduates, junior associates.

Demographics:

  • Age: 22-30
  • Location: Urban
  • Income: Moderate
  • Interests: Academic Support, Job Search

Premium Plan (R300/month)

Target Market: Mid-level associates, senior associates, partners, judges, legal academics, legal entrepreneurs.

Demographics:

  • Age: 30-55+
  • Location: Major Cities
  • Income: High
  • Interests: Leadership, Networking
