Subscription Plans Overview

A comprehensive breakdown of our subscription plans, target demographics, and marketing strategies.

Target Market Demographics

Basic Plan (R100/month)

Target Market: High school students, pre-law undergraduates, career changers.

Income Level: Low to middle-income (R0 - R10,000/month)

Interests: Law, legal studies, career exploration.

Standard Plan (R150/month)

Target Market: Law students, recent graduates, junior associates.

Income Level: Middle-income (R10,000 - R25,000/month)

Interests: Academic excellence, bar exam prep, job search.

Premium Plan (R300/month)

Target Market: Mid-to-senior level professionals, legal entrepreneurs.

Income Level: High-income (R25,000 - R100,000+/month)

Interests: Leadership, business development, networking.
